This movie is about a serial killer who only murders either depressed or suicidal people. The serial killer has killed many victims but this movie mainly tells the story of just one victim. The victim is a depressed woman. The moment the serial killer met this woman, he instantly sensed her depression. The serial killer started stalking the woman. The serial killer added an electronic hacking tool to the woman's car. The tool was a hacking device that allowed the serial killer full remote control over her vehicle. One night the woman was driving on the freeway, the serial killer used his remote control to sabatoge the woman's breaking system, causing the woman to be involved in a car accident.
