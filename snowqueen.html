Long ago there was a demon who enjoyed performing pranks on God's angels. The greatest prank he ever performed was when he invented the magic mirror. The mirror was coated with a special property so that anyone who looked at the mirror would have their world-view permanently altered. The mirror made everything seem ugly and hideous. It is said that the mirror was a reflection of the Devil himself. A group of God's angels gathered around the mirror, each took turns staring at the demon's mirror. Those who stared became suicidal and depressed. Only one angel lived to tell about it, because he glanced quickly while the mirror was not pointing firectly at his eyes in that moment. This angel warned God about the mirror's capability. God ordered his angels to close their eyes. When God looked through the mirror, it made him frown. Noone had ever seen God frown before, but when the demon witnessed God's frown, the demon laughed so much that the heavens shook and the mirror shattered from vibrations.
