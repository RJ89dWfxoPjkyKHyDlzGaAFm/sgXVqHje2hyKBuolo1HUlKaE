Summer 2013 Russian director Kirill Serebrennikov was denied funding for his upcoming documentary on the famously homosexual Russian composer Pyotr Tchaikovsky.

11 June 2013 Russia criminalized media depictions of LGBT. 1842-1885 In Russian Empire homosexuality was punishable by lashing with birch rods, deportation to Siberia and loss of all civil rights. 6 November 1893 Pyotr Tchaikovsky died from Cholera. 26 July 1981 Scholars theorized that Pyotr Tchaikovsky's death was a suicide by self inflicted Cholera.
