As you may know, audacity, arrogance and a love of esotericism (“it’s a great piece
of artwork...you just don’t get it”) are common features of the art world, especially
among privileged and highly connected, younger artists. Therefore, I think it is
plausible that the E-Team might also stand for Explosives Team (amongst
themselves, their handlers and sponsors) and that Gelitin’s The B-Thing may also be,
not so secretly, referring to explosives (see blasting gelatin)
In the years that followed the careers of these two artist groups flourished with shows
all over the world.
