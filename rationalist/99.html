Dov Zakheim
According to the Foreign Policy Research Institute:172
From 1985 until March 1987, Dr. Zakheim was Deputy Under Secretary of Defense for
Planning and Resources in the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Policy). Dr.
Zakheim held several other DOD posts from 1981 to 1985. [Reagan Administration]
Earlier, he was a principal analyst in the National Security and International Affairs
Division of the Congressional Budget Office.
From 1987 to 2001 he was both corporate vice president of System Planning
Corporation, a technology and analysis firm based in Arlington, Va. and chief executive
officer of its subsidiary, SPC International Corp.
From 2001 to April 2004 he was Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) and Chief
Financial Officer for the Department of Defense
