9. How did the fires cause WTC 7 to collapse?
The heat from the uncontrolled fires caused steel floor beams and girders to thermally
expand, leading to a chain of events that caused a key structural column to fail. The
failure of this structural column then initiated a fire-induced progressive collapse of the
entire building.
