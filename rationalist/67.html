In the aftermath of the terrorist attacks on the World Trade Center and the Pentagon on
September 11th, there was widespread speculation that the terrorists or their associates
had used advance knowledge of the attacks to profit in the financial markets.
The paper concludes that, “there is evidence of unusual option market activity in the days leading
up to September 11 that is consistent with investors trading on advance knowledge of the attacks.”
Additionally, CBS News reports in their article, Profiting from Disaster, that on “the afternoon

Sources say they have never seen that kind of imbalance before…An extremely
