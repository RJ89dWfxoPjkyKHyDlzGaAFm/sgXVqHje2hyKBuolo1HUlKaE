Francisco with a microbe, Serratia marcescens, in a simulated germ warfare attack.
It was estimated that the city’s 800,000 residents had each received a heavy dose

From 1950 to 1966, the military performed open-air testing of potential terrorist weapons

and Panama City, FL, exposing still unknown numbers of Americans to Serratia and
