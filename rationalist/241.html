again, evil itself. This is a sophisticated cover-up, yet, as we can see, all of the facts necessary to piece
the picture together are available in the verifiable public record. It is literally physically impossible for 9/11

questioning this, the American public cheered enthusiastically in unison, while outrageously psychopathic
and dishonest politicians robbed them, lied to them, and abused them. They cheered when bombs were

human lives while they greedily milked corrupted and compromised government contracts. Due to the

9/11 was the catalyst that set the precedent for “extraordinary” events to take away our freedom.

in the unfruitful works of darkness, but instead expose them”, and has utterly failed in that regard.
