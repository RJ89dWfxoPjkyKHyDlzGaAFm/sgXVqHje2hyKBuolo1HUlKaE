In the UCSB Presidential Archives, we can examine Executive Order 6102, Requiring Gold Coin,
Gold Bullion and Gold Certificates to Be Delivered to the Government, from April 5, 1933.96
According to the New York Times, in their 1933 article titled, Hoarding of Gold:97
The Executive order issued by the President yesterday amplifies and particularizes his
earlier warnings against hoarding. On March 6, taking advantage of a wartime statute
which had not been repealed, he forbade the hoarding "of gold or silver coin or bullion or
currency," under penalty of $10,000 fine or ten years' imprisonment or both.
