Cee Cee Lyles Phone Call
Before we discuss the alleged phone calls from the planes in greater detail, we will examine a
message from a victim, Cee Cee Lyles, a flight attendant on Flight 93.323 We must do as she says, and
“listen carefully”. Listen closely to what appears to be a whisper in the last 10 seconds after she is done
talking. It may be helpful to repeat it a few times, and headphones are preferable, starting at
approximately 40 seconds:

Lyles appears to whisper at the end, “It’s a frame.” This is a troubling and disturbing development
on top of a truly questionable series of events as told by the government. We must never forget the
massive amount of human suffering that happened on that day, as we strive to discern what really
happened to the people onboard the planes.
