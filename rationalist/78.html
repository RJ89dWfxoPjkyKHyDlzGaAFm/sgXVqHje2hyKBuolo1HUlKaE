Next, let’s look at the FAA Command Center (ATCSCC- Air Traffic Control System
Command Center).
 Ben Sliney, Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) National Operations Manager.
 First day on the job
According to CBS News, On September 11th, Sliney had been on the job for less than one day.140
Nine months later, on Sept. 11, 2001, he took over as boss of the FAA's command center
in Herndon, Va.
We can confirm this from the United States Naval Academy website:141
His first day as National Operations Manager for the Federal Aviation Administration at
the Air Traffic Control System Command Center, which encompasses all the airspace in
the United States, was September 11th, 2001.
