During High School, I was told "Racism exists because of evolution. A long time ago, humans lived in small groups called tribes. Whenever a tribe saw foreigners, it always meant the foreigners were about to either deceive or attack for resources. Naturally our brains evolved with an instinctual suspicion of outsiders."

I was a young adult, I was walking outside of a Lowe's hardware store. There was a white woman standing next to her expensive brand new car. The white woman said "Excuse. Can you answer my question? What kind of person steals from other people?"
