Elliot Smith - Between the Bars
The lyrics personifies addiction. The lyrics "Drink up baby" is referring to the predatory rhetoric of alcoholism upon victims, enticing users to drink by the promise of pleasant possibilities. "Between the Bars" refers to the hours of sobriety. Elliott Smith was eerily stabbed to death possibly from suicide or homicide. This song was referenced in a Rick and Morty episode.

Burgmuller - The Farewell, Op. 100 No. 12
Le Adieu describes the pain of losing someone to death or circumstances. The intro: starts off very slow, sad, bitter, but resolute. The verse: notes are close together in a frustrated manner. The chorus suddenly is very happy which symbolizes bipolarism. The contrast between the verse and chorus, is a battle between happiness and sadness. The outro ends on a bitter note. The next piece, Op. 100 No. 13 is titled Consolation.
