Mr. Hornstra

During middle school I had a Physical Education teacher named Mr. Hornstra. Mr. Hornstra showed us a video of when him with his friends traveled across the country on bicylces. He said "We went ocean to ocean on our bikes. We started on California with our back tire touching the Pacific Ocean. We completed when the front of our bike touched the Atlantic ocean." He used to have long blonde hair. Now he is bald. Mr. Hornstra explained "We bicyled in one straight column, because it was advantageous. You see, the person in front would absorb the impact of the wind, so the rest of us would conserve glycogen. We adjusted our seats based on the angle of our knees."

I had very bad eyesight. One day I wore glasses to middle school for the first time. Mr. Hornstra said to me "I remember the first time I wore prescription glasses. I felt as though someone painted the walls with fresh paint. When I looked at a tree, with all its textures I realized how beautiful life is." Mr. Hornstra always said gay things.

As you earn trust, then you must add a block. When you acquire a frienship, then you must add another block or two. But all it takes is one mistake and everything that you have built together has toppled. It is important to build a strong foundation of trust. A foundation that was founded upon lies or deception is one that is destined to collapse."

Another student interrupted "But what about if you dipped everything in superglue?" Mr. Hornstra said "Now that is just being silly."
