My Biological Sister's First Dog

My biological sister's first dog. It was a long time ago ~2005. The dog got his head stuck underneath a fence. The adults had to remove the fence to help release the dog. My biological father would always carry the dog by pinching the dog's loose skin-folds. I asked my father "Dad why do we carry dogs by it's loose skin-folds?" I replied "Would you atleast carry him with two of your hands?" My father replied "No. Dog's don't feel on their scruffs."

My Biological Father's Dogfighting Bloodsport

I asked my biological father "Is it true that you used to host dogfighting events?" My biological father replied "Yes. I was young and naive. I did what I had to do to earn money. I regret allowing the dogs to fight eachother. I don't condone it anymore." I asked my biological father "Would the dogs fight to their deaths?" My biological father replied "No. I never allowed the dogs to die. The dogs would fight until they were bloody. Some dogs gave up, or the dog owners forfeited, or the judges concluded. But it was never deathly. The dogs were a financial investment. We made gambling wagers. Some dogs gave up, or the dog owners forfeited, or the judges concluded. But it was never deathly. I never made my own dogs fight. Others brought their own dog. I simply hosted the events at my house. I charged a $100 entrance fee per spectator. That was how I earned money. If the cops showed up, I would have been the first one to get arrested because it was my house. Well actually it was your biological mother's house, but the cops never showed up."
